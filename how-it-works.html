Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to start your auction journey

1

Create Account

Sign up for free in under a minute. Provide your basic information and set up your profile to start participating in auctions.

2

Browse Items

Explore hundreds of auctions across multiple categories. Use filters and search to find exactly what you're looking for.

3

Place Bids

Enter your maximum bid amount. Our system will automatically place incremental bids to keep you in the lead up to your limit.

4

Monitor Activity

Track your active bids in real-time. Get notified when you're outbid or when an auction is about to end.

5

Win & Pay

If you're the highest bidder when the auction ends, you win! Complete payment securely through our platform.

6

Receive Item

Coordinate shipping with the seller and receive your winning item. Leave feedback to help the community.

Tips for Winning Bids

Strategies to help you succeed

Bid Late

Place your bids in the final minutes of an auction to avoid driving up the price early. Use the countdown timer to time your entry perfectly.

Set a Budget

Decide your maximum bid before the auction starts. Factor in shipping costs and stick to your budget to avoid overpaying in the heat of the moment.

Research Items

Read descriptions carefully, check seller ratings, and compare prices. Knowledge is power when it comes to successful bidding.
